About Bitcoin Regret

This was a fast-and-loose project that was implemented for fun in December 2017, when Bitcoin reached its peak price.

All Bitcoin prices were gathered from a publicly available history table on Investing.com.

For maximum potential regret, this site uses the high price from each week since February 2012. Before 2012, Bitcoin's price wasn't tracked by sites like Investing.com. However, just remember that Bitcoin was worth basically NOTHING in 2009.

Have a nice day!
