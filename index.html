What is this?

This was a small project that was implemented for fun in December 2017, when Bitcoin reached its peak price.

All Bitcoin prices were gathered from a publicly available history table on Investing.com. The BTC count is rounded to 2 decimal places for formatting purposes.

For maximum potential regret, this site uses the high price point from each week since February 2012. Before 2012, Bitcoin's price wasn't tracked by sites like Investing.com. However, just remember that Bitcoin was worth basically NOTHING in 2009.
